schedule Exchange

Learn about our all schedule
exchange benefits

Schedule Exchange from Payonline helps managers reduce overstaffing, avoid understaffing, control labor costs and meet compliance demands. Its automated functionality provides for a seamless workflow with payroll and time and labor. Cloud-based convenience provides managers an instant, big-picture view on employee availability, and gives employees anytime access to their schedules.

  • Use shift templates and schedule groups to create and edit schedules.
  • Set exception, rounding and deviation rules for your pay classes.
  • Implement a point system in line with company policies.
  • Employees can view their schedule, as well as approve it and move it to their online time sheet.
  • Monitor scheduling changes, daily exceptions other attendance activity.
  • Allocate shifts on a day-to-day basis.
  • Batch-assign shifts to multiple employees.
  • Assigned shifts automatically display on in Employee Self-Service.
  • Co-workers can swap shifts or issue requests for a shift to be picked up.
